Lore Play

Enter the Realm of Living Signs

In Petborn Realms, you are the creature. Your birthdate ties you to an elemental path, and that path shapes the way you move, fight, and grow across the board.

The Realm as a World

Petborn Realms is a single shared world. Realms are not places you travel to; they are the ways energy flows through you. Your sign, your element, and your chosen form decide how you survive the board.
